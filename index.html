🗺️ Gatlinburg Adventure – July 21–23

Chad & Co.'s Mountain Escape

Day 1 – Monday, July 21

  • 🚗 Depart Santa Claus, IN at 5:00 AM (Central)
  • 🦫 Stop at Buc-ee’s in Sevierville, TN for lunch (~11:30 AM EST)
  • 🎢 Visit Dollywood from 12:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • 🍴 Dinner and cinnamon bread at Spotlight Bakery inside Dollywood
  • 🏨 Check into Tru by Hilton – Pigeon Forge (after 3:00 PM)

Day 2 – Tuesday, July 22

  • 🥞 Free hotel breakfast
  • 🎟️ Explore all Ripley's Attractions in Gatlinburg (Aquarium, Believe It or Not, Mirror Maze, etc.)
  • 🛍️ Walk through The Village Shops
  • 🌲 Moderate hike to Cataract Falls (~1 mile RT)
  • 🍕 Lunch at Slice Pizza Bakery
  • 🥩 Dinner at Cherokee Grill
  • 🎥 Catch the evening ride at SkyFly: Soar America

Day 3 – Wednesday, July 23

  • 📦 Check out early, bring breakfast/snacks
  • 🚘 Scenic drive through Cades Cove Loop (start here) — bring cameras!
  • 🥪 Picnic lunch (grab supplies night before)
  • 💧 Hike to Spruce Flats Falls (1.4 mile RT)
  • 🍔 Dinner stop at Culver’s in Bowling Green, KY
  • 🏡 Return home to Santa Claus, IN by 11:00 PM CST
